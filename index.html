Demand Scoring Formula

Score = (unique_customers × 5) + (total_mentions × 1) + (enterprise_signals × 5) + (escalation_signals × 5) + (renewal_risk_signals × 10)

Confidence is intentionally independent of the demand score. A feature with high demand but low confidence means the signal is strong in volume but comes from few or unverified sources. A feature with low demand but high confidence means the signal is weak in volume but very well-validated. Both dimensions are needed to correctly prioritize investments.
